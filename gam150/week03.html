This session will look at further developing your Unity platform game creation skills through working with graphics to created animated components, larger levels and linking levels together to create longer-term progression

Learning Objectives:
  • Create larger and more complex game levels and link levels together
  • Develop approaches for implementing 'key & lock' challenges
  • Use Unity to trigger audio at specific events
