This session marks the point at which the focus shifts from theory to practice. We will begin with a short introduction to communication protocols. The rest of the session will be dedicated to locking down the bill of materials (BOM) for your project.

Learning Objectives:
  • List the communication protocols that Arduino supports
  • Explain the strengths and weaknesses of each protocol
  • Create a bill of materials specific to your project requirements
