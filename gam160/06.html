This session introduces you to the basics of the Arduino platform. Further to this, you learn to work with components to create basic interface. This will lay the foundation for how to create your own designs using Arduino.

Learning Objectives:
  • Identify the parts of the Arduino and their purpose
  • Write sketches and upload them to the Arduino board
  • Apply the basics to create more complex relationships between sensors and actuators
