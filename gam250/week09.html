This session introduces you to basic of using protocols to communicate with digital sensors from the Arduino. You will be introduced to I2C and SPI, aloong with some other very uesful protocols. You will then be expected to continue the development of your assigmnent tasks.

Learning Objectives:
  • Identify the various protocols used to communicate with devices attached to the Arduino
  • Explain the difference between asynchronous and synchronous communication
  • Outline the strengths and weaknesses of each protocol
